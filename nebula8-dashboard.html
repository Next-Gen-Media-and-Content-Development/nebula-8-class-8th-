🔬 Key Concepts at a Glance
⚗️
Solute + Solvent = Solution
When a solute dissolves in a solvent, they form a uniform mixture called a solution.
🪣
Saturated vs Unsaturated
A saturated solution can hold no more solute; an unsaturated one can dissolve more.
🌡️
Solubility & Temperature
Most solids dissolve more at higher temperatures; gases dissolve less when heated.
🐠
Dissolved Oxygen
Aquatic life depends on oxygen dissolved in water. Warm water holds less of it.
⚖️
Density
Density = Mass ÷ Volume. Denser objects sink; less dense ones float.
🧊
Floating & Sinking
Ice floats on water because it is less dense than liquid water — a life-saving property!
